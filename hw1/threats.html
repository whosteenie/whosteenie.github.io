TRENDS

Cyber threats come in various forms, each exploiting different weaknesses in systems or human behavior. Malware, for instance, refers to harmful software like viruses that can infect devices, steal data, or disrupt operations by spreading through networks or downloads.
SOURCE

Phishing attacks trick users into revealing sensitive information through deceptive emails or websites that mimic legitimate sources. These often lead to identity theft or unauthorized account access. Ransomware, another major threat, encrypts files and demands payment for decryption, causing significant downtime for businesses and individuals.
SOURCE

Other risks include denial-of-service attacks, which overwhelm servers to make services unavailable, and insider threats from within organizations. Understanding these helps in building defenses, as seen in high-profile incidents affecting companies and governments worldwide.
SOURCE
