To combat cyber risks, organizations and individuals employ a variety of measures and tools. Firewalls act as barriers, monitoring and controlling incoming and outgoing network traffic based on security rules to block unauthorized access.
SOURCE

Encryption transforms data into unreadable formats for anyone without the proper keys, ensuring secure transmission over the internet. Antivirus and anti-malware software scan for and remove threats, often using real-time monitoring to detect suspicious activity.
SOURCE

Best practices include regular software updates to patch vulnerabilities, multi-factor authentication to add layers of verification, and employee training to recognize social engineering tactics. Tools like intrusion detection systems further enhance protection by alerting to potential breaches in progress.
