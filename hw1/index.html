Cybersecurity involves safeguarding computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, damage, or theft. In today's interconnected world, where everything from personal devices to critical infrastructure relies on digital technology, protecting against cyber risks is essential. It encompasses a range of practices designed to maintain the integrity of information.
SOURCE

The field has evolved rapidly with the growth of the internet and cloud computing. Key aspects include identifying vulnerabilities, implementing defenses, and responding to incidents. Professionals in this area work to prevent attacks that could lead to financial losses and privacy breaches. As technology advances, cybersecurity adapts to new challenges, making it a dynamic and crucial part of computer science.
